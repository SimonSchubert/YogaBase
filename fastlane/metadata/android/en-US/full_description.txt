YogaBase is an offline yoga companion with voice-guided sessions, illustrated poses, and a daily streak to keep you moving. Pick a session category, choose your pace, and let the app take you through each pose.

Features:
- 25 guided session categories across beginner, intermediate, and advanced
- 48 illustrated yoga poses with English, Sanskrit, and translated names
- Voice narration for every pose plus an intro countdown and mid-pose side switch
- Three pacing modes: Swift, Balanced, and Endurance
- Daily streak tracking that persists across sessions
- Works fully offline — no account required
- Available on Android, iOS, Desktop, and Web
